Decent Living Standard « WGIII »

AR6

A set of minimal material requirements essential for achieving basic human well-being including nutrition, shelter, basic living conditions, clothing, healthcare, education, and mobility (Rao and Baer 2012; Rao and Min 2018; O’Neill et al. 2018).

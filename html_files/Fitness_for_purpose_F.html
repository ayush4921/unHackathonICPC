Fitness-for-purpose « WGI »

AR6

The suitability of a model (or other resource, such as a dataset or method) for a particular task, such as quantifying the contribution of increased greenhouse gas concentrations to recent changes in global mean surface temperature or projecting changes in drought frequency in a region under a given scenario. Assessment of a model’s fitness-for-purpose can be informed both by how the model represents relevant physical processes and by how it scores on relevant performance metrics.

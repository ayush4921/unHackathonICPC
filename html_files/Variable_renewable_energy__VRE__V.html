Variable renewable energy (VRE) « WGIII »

AR6

Renewable energy sources such as wind and solar energy whose output is determined by weather, in contrast to ‘dispatchable’ generators that adjust their output as a reaction to economic incentives. Variable renewables have also been termed intermittent, fluctuating, or non-dispatchable. (Hirth, 2013)

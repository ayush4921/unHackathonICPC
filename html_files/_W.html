Wind energy « WGIII »

AR6

Kinetic energy from airflow arising from the uneven heating of the Earth’s surface. The wind’s kinetic energy is converted to mechanical shaft energy and electricity by a wind turbine, a rotating machine. A wind farm, wind project, wind park, or wind power plant is a group of wind turbines interconnected to a common utility system through a system of transformers, distribution lines, and (usually) one substation.
